# Privacy Policy for MemoryFlow

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026
**Effective Date:** January 3, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to MemoryFlow! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use MemoryFlow (the "App").

By using MemoryFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use MemoryFlow, you provide the following information:

- **Learning Topics and Content**: Text content, notes, and study materials you create or import into the app
- **Tags and Categories**: Organizational labels you assign to your topics
- **Review History**: Records of when you reviewed topics and your progress through spaced repetition stages
- **User Preferences**: App settings including theme preferences, notification settings, font size, and color scheme choices
- **Custom Schedules**: Any custom review schedules you set for specific topics

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App automatically collects and stores:

- **Review Statistics**: Data about your study patterns, review counts, stage progression, and completion rates
- **App Usage Data**: Information about which features you use and how you interact with the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility purposes)
- **Timestamps**: Creation dates, review dates, and last modified dates for your content

### 1.3 Third-Party Services (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

- **Google Account Information**: Email address associated with your Google account (used only for authentication and backup purposes)
- **Google Drive Access**: Limited access to create and read backup files in your own Google Drive storage

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- **Spaced Repetition Algorithm**: To calculate optimal review intervals and schedule notifications
- **Progress Tracking**: To display your learning statistics and progress over time
- **Content Management**: To organize and retrieve your study materials
- **Personalization**: To apply your preferred themes, settings, and customizations

### 2.2 Notifications
- **Review Reminders**: To send local notifications when topics are due for review
- **Daily Reminders**: To notify you based on your configured reminder time
- **Overdue Alerts**: To alert you about topics that are past their review date (if enabled)

### 2.3 Backup and Sync (Optional)
- **Data Backup**: To create and restore backups of your learning data to your Google Drive
- **Data Recovery**: To help you restore your content if you change devices or reinstall the app

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage

MemoryFlow is designed as a **privacy-first, offline-first application**:

- **Local Database**: All your learning content, topics, and review history are stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database
- **No Cloud Servers**: We do not store your data on any servers owned or operated by us
- **Offline Functionality**: The app works completely offline without requiring an internet connection

### 3.2 Google Drive Storage (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

- **Your Google Drive**: Backup files are stored in **your own Google Drive account**, not on our servers
- **Encrypted Backups**: Backup data is stored in JSON format with timestamps
- **User Control**: You have full control over backup files in your Google Drive and can delete them at any time
- **No Access to Other Files**: We only access backup files created by MemoryFlow; we cannot access any other files in your Google Drive

### 3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Local Encryption**: Data stored on your device is protected by your device's security features
- **Secure Authentication**: Google Drive integration uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
- **No Data Transmission**: Your learning content is never transmitted to any third-party servers (except your own Google Drive if you enable backup)
- **Permission Controls**: The app requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its functionality

### 4.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We **do not sell, trade, rent, or share** your personal information or learning content with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Data Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View Your Data**: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
- **Edit Your Data**: You can modify or delete any topics, notes, or content you've created
- **Export Your Data**: You can back up your data to your Google Drive in JSON format
- **Delete Your Data**: You can delete individual topics or clear all app data through your device settings

### 5.2 Notification Controls

You have full control over notifications:

- **Enable/Disable**: Turn notifications on or off at any time in app settings
- **Customize Timing**: Set your preferred reminder times
- **Quiet Hours**: Configure quiet hours to prevent notifications during specific times
- **Permission Revocation**: Revoke notification permissions through your device settings

### 5.3 Google Drive Integration

You can control Google Drive integration:

- **Connect/Disconnect**: Enable or disable Google Drive backup at any time
- **Revoke Access**: Disconnect your Google account through the app settings
- **Delete Backups**: Delete backup files directly from your Google Drive
